What is SILEKSI?

SILEKSI is a website to calculate energy usage of electronic devices in a house based on the electricity capacity. SILEKSI also recommends many energy saving products for your house.

Benefits

With SILEKSI, you can calculate the energy usage of your electronic devices in your house, so it can determine whether it's optimal or not based on your house electricity capacity. SILEKSI also gives you many energy saving products recommendation so you can save more energy.
